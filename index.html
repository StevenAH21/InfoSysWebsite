Our Mission

The Information Systems Society promotes the field of Information Systems by building bridges between students and the professional world. We believe that understanding how technology drives business is essential in today's economy.

Through alumni panels, company visits, social events, and professional development workshops, we provide members with the network and knowledge they need to succeed—no matter their major.
