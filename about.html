About ISS

We connect students to the business technology world — through real events, real companies, and real people.

Who We Are

The Information Systems Society is a student-run organization at the Wisconsin School of Business dedicated to bridging the gap between IS education and the professional world.

We create opportunities that your classes can't — direct access to companies, alumni, and peers who are working through the same questions you are. If you're interested in how technology shapes modern business, there's a place for you here.
